Your right to deletion: You can request deletion of your DartForge account and all associated data at any time. Once processed, this action is permanent and cannot be undone.
01

What Gets Deleted

Submitting this request will permanently delete the following data associated with your account:

  • Your player profile — display name, avatar, friend code, and region
  • All match statistics — scores, averages, checkouts, and lifetime history
  • Ranked progression — DR rating, tier, season history, and placement records
  • XP, badges, and level progress
  • Purchased cosmetic entitlements — voice packs, board skins (see note below)
  • Your Firebase Authentication account
⚠ Note on purchases: Deleting your account will remove your entitlement records from DartForge. Google Play purchase records are managed by Google and are not deleted by us. Pro unlocks and cosmetic IAP purchases cannot be refunded through this process — contact Google Play support for refund requests.
