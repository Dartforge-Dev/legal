Common Questions

  • Does my Season 0 rank count for real? Yes. Your DR, wins, losses, and season history from Season 0 are permanent records.
  • Will I lose my progress if I don't subscribe at Season 1? No. Your account, XP, and badges are all retained. You just won't be able to play ranked online until you subscribe.
  • Can I still use DartForge for free after Season 0? Yes. Local X01 and Cricket scoring, the XP system, badges, and live match stats are free forever.
  • Does the subscription include Pro features? Yes. An active subscription gives you full access to everything — training suite, lifetime stats, cloud sync, QR share, and ranked online play. You don't need to buy Pro separately.
  • How will I know when Season 1 is starting? You will see an in-app banner at least two weeks before the change. We will not flip the switch without clear advance notice.
  • I already bought Pro — do I need a subscription for online? Pro gives you a 30-day free trial of online play. After that, yes — a subscription is required. The subscription also includes all Pro features so there's no overlap in cost if you subscribe.
